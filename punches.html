Punches

The punches are commonly used marking tool in fitting shop for marking points on work pieces.It consists of steel rod with sharpened point.Hardening and Tempeing is done to its point head.The midle part of punch is made suitable to hold easily and called body.Mostly the portion of body has knurling surface for better grip.Punches are normally 90 to 150mm long and 8 to 13mm in diameter

Types of Punches

  1. Prick punch
  2. Center punch
  3. Dot punch
  4. Bell punch
