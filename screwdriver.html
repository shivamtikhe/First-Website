Screwdriver

The screwdrivers are used to remove or refit headed screw in workshop.It contains blade or head,body and handle.There are two types of screwdriver according to type of blade,flat blade and Phillips head screwdriver.Screwdrivers are also classified as light duty and heavy duty according to their use.Screwdrivers are made to various lengths and thickness to suit the corresponding size and slots on the screw head.Now a days handles of screwdrives are made from heavy duty industrial plastic sometimes wooden or ebonite handles also used.The body of screwdrivers used for electrical work is coated with insulator sleeves

Types of Screwdrives

  1. Flat head screwdrivers
  2. Phillips head screwdrivers
